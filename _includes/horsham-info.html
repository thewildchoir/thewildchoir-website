Sessions are lead by Natural Voice Choir leader Philippa Snell and run during term times. Philippa firmly believes that everyone with a voice can sing and that making music together is connecting, uplifting and good for the soul.

Each term will have 6 weeks of 'Drop in' sessions where anyone can try the choir out, then the following block of 6/7 weeks will be booking only and we will work towards a performance (discount for booking the whole term in advance).
